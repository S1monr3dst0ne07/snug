Objs

Objs (Objects) in the compiler are the semantic units of which the language is made up of.
They are registered under compiler/objs.py, are read by the tree.parse head using hasattr/getattr, and are therefore dynamic.

Interface

All classes under objs.py oblige themself to implement at least 2 and at most 3 methods.

Necessary

 
            parse   (self: type(self), stream: streamer)                -> type(self)
            generate(self: type(self), output: output, ctx: _rout._ctx) -> void

Optional

            infer   (self: type(self), ctx: _rout._ctx) -> void
